Despite being less than a decade old, Kubernetes is a buzzword for many in and around the cloud infrastructure space. The word "Kubernetes" is Greek for "Helmsman", but what does Kubernetes actually do? Are Kubernetes and Docker the same thing? What is and is not so great about it? And does your organization need it? This talk is both an introduction to Kubernetes, as well as a discussion on what Kubernetes can bring to your platform -- the good, the bad, and the complex!
