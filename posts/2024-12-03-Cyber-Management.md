Cyber risk and security are often synonymous, but they are two different concepts when defining and measuring Enterprise IT operations. This talk defines methods and application for cyber risk and cyber security practices. The discussion includes how DevOps, Software Development, Data center, Operations, and Data Governance support the measurement and reporting of cyber at all levels of the organization, and how you can use or develop metrics to better manage with your team. The presentation will be interactive and look to audience members to participate.
