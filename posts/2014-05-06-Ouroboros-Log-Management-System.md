In this presentation, Shane will describe his experiences in the design and deployment of Ouroboros, a log management system for a large enterprise environment. General topics shall include requirements definition, system design, implementation, and monitoring. Components will include Linux, rsyslog, heartbeat and corosync, GlusterFS, Ceph, Splunk, Hadoop, LogStash, and various tools and methods for monitoring and performance.
